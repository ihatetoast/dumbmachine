A Dumb Doldrumachine

Two years ago, I took Wes Bos's JavaScript30 challenge. While completing each day, I wrote down notes of how I'd do my own version of each day. Then I let two years go by. Instead of a drum kit, I created an old keyboard with random sounds I made. I was in state of ennui and found myself entertained making silly sounds. The Dumb Doldrumachine was born.

Use your keys to annoy your friends, Romans, countrymen, and pets.
